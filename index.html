I'm Filip, [1]

a Filipino-Norwegian with a passion for volleyball, fitness, and programming.

I focus on creating digital experiences, developing games, and software, bringing ideas to life through code.

Outside of programming, I spend time in the gym, on the volleyball court, and keeping up with the latest in technology.

Welcome to my portfolio.

In 2019, I started programming with Microsoft MakeCode Arcade, building some simple games like a small dungeon crawler, and some other small rpg games. This period really sparked my interest in coding.

By 2021, I had developed a passion for game development and started working on small projects using the Godot engine, diving deeper into developing interactive experiences and games.

In the summer of 2022, I enrolled at Kirkeparken videregående skole and chose computer science as my school program. In computer science, we focused on web development and built up both front-end and back-end skills.

In 2024, I have delved deeper into programming, driven by my thirst for knowledge. This journey eventually led me to start learning C++. Currently, I am developing my own compiler from the ground up.
